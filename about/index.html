About

ASPECTMOBILI

aspectmobili — это проект в сфере пространственного дизайна от HITOBA DESIGN, нацеленный на обогащение людей и пространств вместе с нашими клиентами.

Scroll
(01)

Concept

We create spaces that are not just offices, but fragments of architectural intelligence.

The world of work is evolving. At aspectmobili, we believe the physical environment is a catalyst for culture. Our approach merges minimal aesthetics with structural precision, resulting in spaces that breathe and inspire.

By focusing on "Spatial Fragments", we design components that work in harmony to support productivity and brand identity.

(02)

Philosophy

Design is a dialogue between human needs and architectural limits.

We leverage advanced engineering to push the boundaries of spatial possibility, ensuring every project is a timeless fragment of excellence.

  1. (Spatial Intelligence)

    Designing beyond the surface.

    We believe that great office design starts with understanding the people who use it. Through deep analysis of workflows, culture, and business goals, we uncover the essence of what a space truly needs to become.

  2. (Order Made)

    A bespoke team for every challenge.

    No two projects are the same. We assemble a dedicated team of designers, engineers, and craftspeople tailored to each project's unique requirements, collaborating with external artists and specialists when the vision demands it.

  3. (One Goal. Infinite Ways)

    One destination. Endless paths.

    In the creative domain, we refuse to be confined by convention. With a clear objective shared among the entire team, we explore every angle, every possibility, crafting solutions that are as inventive as they are functional.
