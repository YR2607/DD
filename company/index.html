Design Philosophy

Every space tells a story. We decode the narrative of organizations and translate it into architectural language that resonates with people who inhabit it daily.

02

Spatial Fragments

Complex environments broken into meaningful components. Each fragment designed to foster creativity, well-being, and purposeful interaction between teams.

03

Future Forward

Hybrid work demands adaptive spaces. We pioneer solutions where physical and digital realms merge, creating workplaces that evolve with their users.

(04)

History

2018

Foundation

HITOBA DESIGN established in Shibuya, Tokyo. First project completed within 3 months of launch, setting the pace for future growth.

2019

Expansion

Team grows to 15 members. Launched the aspectmobili brand as a spatial design platform connecting architecture with human experience.

2021

100 Projects Milestone

Reached 100 completed office design projects across the Tokyo metropolitan area. Recognized as a leading force in workspace transformation.

2023

Award Recognition

Multiple design awards received. Portfolio expanded to include comprehensive branding services alongside spatial design.

2025

Beyond Boundaries

150+ projects completed. Pioneering sustainable and hybrid workspace solutions for the next generation of work environments.
